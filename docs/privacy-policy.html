Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Zazen Timer (“the App”) is a meditation timer designed for Wear OS devices. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how information is handled.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

No user accounts are required. No personal data is requested.

Health and Meditation Data

The App does not collect or store meditation sessions, health data, or biometric information outside of the device. Any settings or session information remain locally on your device.

Internet Connectivity

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.
